About Us

Demo Site Technologies has a span history of growth and success. Founded in 1996, Demo Site started off as an IT consulting partner for one of the most recognized brands in the agricultural equipment manufacturing industry. With steady and planned growth, Demo Site’s vision went global in the year 2000, with introduction of offshore operations in Indore, India. Named as of one of the “Fast 500 companies”, Demo Site has grown organically through strategic acquisitions. We currently have over 4000 global employees, and growing.

Our core values of customer centricity, agility and innovation haven’t changed, but the depth and breadth of our service offerings and value that we deliver to our customers certainly has. Today, Demo Site is a well-regarded, global IT service provider, with customers in a number of industries. Demo Site provides a comprehensive portfolio of services and solutions that not only solve today’s IT challenges, but also address tomorrow’s business priorities. As a strategic premier application and infrastructure outsourcing services provider, Demo Site enables our customers to maximize the value of their IT investments, while helping them optimize their IT expenses for sustained business value. As a specialist in delivering business transformation through IT, we partners with our customers to help drive positive outcomes. This unique engagement approach, coupled with the ability to execute with speed and nimbleness, has ensured that Demo Site is the partner of choice for hundreds of global corporations, across six continents.

Our employees are the key to the company’s exceptional client service and industry-leading growth. The nurturing and challenging environment within the organization provides our employees with opportunities to build successful careers and actualize themselves. In 2016, “AON Hewitt” recognized Demo Site to be among the Top 25 employers in India (across industries) and “Great Place to Work” named us among the Top 50. The Demo Site Foundation contributes towards supporting the underprivileged, as part of a dedicated effort towards community development.
